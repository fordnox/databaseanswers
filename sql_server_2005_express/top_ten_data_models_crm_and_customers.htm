A set of CRM and Customers Data Models.
 
1 CRM Includes Customers, Personalisation, Channels, Knowledge Base and Data Warehouse.
2 Customers and Addresses Includes Addresses, Address Types, Contact Channels, Customers, Orders and Products.
3 Customers and Campaigns Includes Campaigns, Contacts, Customers, Orders, Products and Suppliers.
4 Customers and Deliveries Includes Addresses, Customers, Deliveries, Employees, Orders, Routes and Trucks.
5 Customers and Department Stores Includes Addresses, Customers, Departments, Jobs, Products, Purchases and Suppliers.
6 Customers and Insurance Includes Claims, Customers, Payments and Policies.
7 Customers and Invoices Includes Accounts, Customers, Invoices, Orders, Products and Transactions.
8 Customers and Orders Includes Customers, Invoices, Orders, Order Items and Products.
9 Customers and Order Tracking Includes Customers, Invoices, Orders, Order Items, Products and Shipments.
10 Data Warehouse DW for Customers and Purchases, includes Dimensions of Customers, Geography, Products and Time Periods.
