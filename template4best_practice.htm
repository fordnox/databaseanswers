A Template for defining a Best Practice Case study
 

This page defines a standard Template which can be used for describing a Best Practice Case Study.
Here are two examples :-

  1. Guidelines laid down by the UN AIDS Agency,
  2. Guidelines for making a Submission to the Best Practice Awards, sponsored by UN-HABITAT and Dubai.

There will be an entry in the Infomation_Catalog table for each Best Practice which is available.

The information displayed on this page is taken from these tables in the GCI Database :-
    Manuals, Chapters,Sections, Paragraphs and Images.

  • This Page shows fields from the Chapters Table are :-
    1. chapter_code, chapter_title (eg 1. Title), printed in chapter_number sequence.
    2. chapter_text (eg This gives information about ...).
