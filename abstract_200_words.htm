B. Functional Specifications

There are three areas to the functional requirements

1)       Performance Monitoring

This reports on Student Attendance in a Training College and displays the results in an Intranet Portal window for the Senior Manager responsible.

 

2)       Best Practice

A lesson, such as installing a Windows Operating System’ can be an online Tutorial.

 

3)       User Portals and Communities

User Portals make it possible to deliver a tailored personalized service to individual Users – students, teachers, administrators, and so on.

In addition, students can join Communities that reflect their interests and make contact with kindred spirits, such as Greek students, fans of Chelsea football club, and so on.
