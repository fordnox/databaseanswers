Data Publication Tim Berners-Lee's involvement has produced this new UK Site entitled Unlocking Innovation that aims to make a wide range of Government data in a way that people can develop their own Data Sets for their particular interests.
Data Publication Google has a similar idea, but on a global scale, which they call Public Data Explorer.
Data has been supplied by a number of organisations, including the OECD, Eurostat, the US Center for Disease Control and the US Bureau of Economic Analysis. It is primarily aimed at students, journalists and researchers, and provides "Data visualisations for a changing world".
You can use it to find statistics on minimum wages, mortality rates, and the cost of living from countries all over the world. and compare them against each other graphically.
This report was taken from an article in the WebUser magazine published on 25 March 2010.
