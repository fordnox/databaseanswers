What's New in Business Intelligence ?

  •  On November 18th. 2010 USA Today contained an interesting report on current developments in BI and how BI software was becoming very user-friendly.
This is a big step forward when it is combined with the availablility of large amounts of free data from places like Google and Microsoft and the US and UK Governments

It presents some exciting new wpossibilities for imaginatuive application of BI.
For example, a Children's Care Home in Las Vegas was able to quality for a $7 million Grant from 
the US Federal Government by using IBM Cognos BI software to keep track of money spent on people in their care.
