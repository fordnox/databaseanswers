Background to design of the CRM Logical Level 
THE LOGICAL LEVEL
The Top-Level specification of the CRM Data Architecture defines the 
Functional Areas and the data flows between the Areas.
The next level down consists of Entities in a Data Model, with one 
Model for each Functional Area, together with Interface definitions. 
A representative set of Interfaces is defined in order to show the 
approach and to give some examples.

In a CRM System, the CRM Application will be the obvious candidate to be the 
Owner for Customer-related Data, and will also be the central point around 
which other Applications will be grouped.

The design of the Logical Level will be presented as a Top-Level overview, supplemented with :-

  • Data Models

  • Data Interface Definitions

  • Data Dictionary to hold a Glossary of Terms

  • Definitions of Data Flows.
