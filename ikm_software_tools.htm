Indigenous Knowledge Management Software Tools
 

An analysis of some of the Tools which are available to record Indigenous Knowledge Management.
1) DatabaseAnswers.org - a free 'Best Practice' Management System.
2) University of Queensland - a mostly free Toolkit specifically for IKM.
3) iUpload - combines Content Management and Blogging.
4) KnowledgeBase - a commercial product offering a wide range of facilities, mostly not appropriate - a similar commercial product.
