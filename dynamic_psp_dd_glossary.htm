  • The top half contains description and narrative text.
  • The lower half shows details of the specific type of Information.

    Glossary of Terms
    TERM SOURCE DEFINITION 
    Ad-hoc Analysis
    		 DBA Analysis performed based on business queries, which are outside standard reporting requirements.
    Global Compact UN A personal initiative of Kofi Annan calling on commmercial organizations to embrace nine universal principles in the three areas of environment, human rights and labour standards.
    Governance CSR 'Doing Things Right', particularly in regard to guidelines such as the IAS or UNGC.
    GRI UN 'Global Reporting Initiave' supports the UN Global Compact and provides guidelines for Sustainability Reporting with Core Key Performance Indicators.
    KPIs   Key Performance Indicators for monitoring Enterprise Performance.
    Operational Risk Basel The risk of loss resulting from inadequate or failed processes and systems, or from external events
    Project CSR A complex Task, involving many people, with a start and end date.
    Users Admin An End-User or non-IT person, such as Compliance Officer or Project Manager.
