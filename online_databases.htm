Online Databases
This is a list of Online Databases that we have checked recently.
They mostly offer only free options, and are therefore likely to be withdrawn, or change their offerings from time to time. We would be grateful if you could let us know if you have any good or bad experiences with any of these Web Sites,(or any others, of course).
You might also be interested in checking out the Applications Rental Guide, which says it is the " most comprehensive guide to Application Service Providers (ASPs) and renting software applications over the web".
NAME COMMENT
  • BasePortal
    		• Free Trial of a "Web-based Database", and also offers a Directory of free Applications submitted by BasePortal Users.
  • eCriteria
    		• Looks good - offers a Database Publishing Wizard, and a free 30-day trial, then $20/month.
  • eUnify
    		• Please try it and let me know what you think - offers a Database Wizard, and a free 14-day trial.
  • Free SQL.org
    		• This service offers mySQL only,(right now), AND is the work of one very enthusiastic individual, who says he offers "Free Database Hosting for developers" - currently running over 5,000 FREE MySQL databases,(May 2004). He may include Oracle in the future.
  • InfoDome
    		•  This Web Site says "Power without Pain". Prices range from Free to $150/month
  • Intranets.com
    		•  This Web Site offers online databases as part of a wider service aimed at small business users.
  • NetMemberServices
    		•  This Web Site offers a range of services.
  • OnlineDatabases
    		•  From Whole Systems Design, Inc. in Lorton, Virginia. The Web Site consists of only one page with a link to a 'Request Form' that doesn't work.
    If it works for you, please let me know.
  • QuickBase
    		• Acquired by Intuit - "Easy Database sharing", says PC Magazine.
    Free Trial, then $249/month for 10 Users, offers a range of predefined solutions for HR, Marketing and so on.
  • Rent-a-DB
    		•  offers a FREE service with a range of predefined Database, or you can design your own, BUT their Databases are really just one table each.
  • Web-To-DB Interface
    		•  A one-man FREE Product from Bo Frese, based in Denmark.
  • Zoho Creator
    		• One satisfied User says "I don't know of another alternative that allows non-coders to create such beautifully easy online databases"
    A Mobile version is also available.
