Data Management Road Map for Vehicle Manufacturers


Our Approach includes the following Steps :-
  • Step 1. Establish a thin slice from top to bottom for a Proof-of-Concept.

  • Step 2. Choose a few Key Performance Indicators, ('KPIs')
    For example, sales as a percentage of population.

  • Step 3. Design the Data Marts for the KPIs
    These are based on a Generic Design with Dimensions and Facts.

  • Step 4. Identify the Data Sources required to provide the data for the KPIs.

  • Step 5. Define details of the Data Integration.
    This will include Data Quality and a Common Data Model with Master Data Management.
