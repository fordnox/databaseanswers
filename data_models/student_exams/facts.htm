FACTS for the Data Model for Students and Exams
  
Addition on 3rd. March 2002 for school/college timetabling database system  :-

Main Components :-

  • teacher/lecturer

  • course

  • subject

  • room

  • time. 

The problem is time as lessons can have different start times, lengths and finish times.
    • Things of Interest in the Scope of the Data Mode :-
      1. Exams.
      2. Courses.
      3. Semesters.
      4. Students.
    • What are the relationships between these Things of Interest :-
      1. A COURSE has one EXAM at the end of each SEMESTER.
      2. An EXAM is always associated with a COURSE and a SEMESTER.
      3. An EXAM is taken at the end of a SEMESTER.
      4. An EXAM can be taken by zero or many STUDENTs.
      5. A STUDENT can take zero or many EXAMS.
    • What are the characteristics of these Things of Interest :-
      1. An EXAM has a DATE.
      2. A SEMESTER has a Start and End Date.
      3. A STUDENT has a Name, and Class.
