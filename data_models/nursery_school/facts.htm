Specifications for the Data Model for a Nursery School 
Back to the Home Page 

From Gabriele 
I’ve found your website looking for existing MS Access databases. 

May I explain you my need, so you can tell me if you have a database ready for this use already done? 

If not, do you think you could help me in getting it?

A friend of mine is working on a Nursery school and, since she has too many kids (from 2 to 6 years old), 
she would need a database to easily handle some information, day by day.

First of all, at the database startup, she needs a search box where to find and select the kid (or enter 
a new one, or delete an old one).

Then, she needs two big buttons “ENTER” and “EXIT”, to automatically get stored the entering time and 
leaving time for each kid.

Default color for this buttons should be grey but, once they have been activated for every single day, 
they have to change color (green would be perfect) to visually give the information the kid has entered/leaved the Nursery School.

Last, she needs two more fields to be completed:
- lunch (if the kid daily eats, yes/no, and a linked note field)
- poop (done yes/no), and a linked note field to put details...

All these info have to be stored and checked every time it’s needed.

In the kid information detail file, they would also store some information as:
- kid’s general information (name, surname, birth date, age, address, contact person 1, contact person 2, 
  contact person 3, picture, some customizable note fields for intolerances, disturbs, problems, activities…)
- purchased hours (budget)
- remaining hours (budget - hours spent in the nursery school)
- count of lunches during the current month
- debits for extra activities (gym - English lessons - logopedyst…)

Do you think that something close to this database request is already available. 
If not, could you help us in getting it, customized for my friend’s Nursery School.
