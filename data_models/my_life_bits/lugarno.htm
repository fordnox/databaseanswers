Getting Started with MLB
 
Barry and Norlia have just returned from a holiday in Lugarno, Switzerland.
This page shows the results of using the holiday as a trial run for following the MyLifeBits Approach to document everything possible.
I used my cell/mobile phone, which is a standard off-the-shelf Internet-enabled Nokia, with Camera and Video.
We used the camera to photo documents instead of using a scanner.

This page reflects the approach in my Data Model for a Library of Digital Assets.

This Page is intended to promote discussion.

The next Steps are :-
  1. Create Templates for Data Entry
  2. Display available Digital Assets ('Bits')
  3. Provide Search facilities.
