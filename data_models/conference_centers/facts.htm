A Database is to be designed to support the operation of a Conference Center.

This is the User specification of the Summary Requirements :-
You are in charge of creating an Information System for TürkiyEM'09 (The national conference for Industrial Engineers of Turkey).

The information system should use a web page as a front-end and through this page both the participants and the organizers will be 
able to join the Information System through their password controlled accounts. 

The participants should be able to register, get information, upload their papers and abstracts. 

The organizers should be able to join at various categories of rights to upkeep the IS
