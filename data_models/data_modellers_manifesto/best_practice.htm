Best Practice in Data Modelling 


Best Practice.

Here is our current Steps involved :-
  1. Create an online Glossary of Terms
  2. Bottom-up Approach - Identify the 'Things of Interest' involved.
  3. Determine the Relationships between the 'Things of Interest'.
  4. Follow Naming Standards -
    1. Primary Keys with Code fields for Reference Data
    2. Names beginning wih REF_ for Reference Data Entities
    3. Primary Keys with Auto-Increment ID fields.
  5. Look for suitable Generic Models
  6. Look for suitable Subject Area Models
  7. Review appropriate Tutorials
  8. Look for suitable Enterprise Data Models
  9. Look for suitable Industry Sector Models
