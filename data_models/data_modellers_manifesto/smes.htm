Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) 


SMEs play a very important part in the success of Data Models within an enterprise.

They often take responsibility for signing-off Data Models, that they have contributed to.

This helps to ensure their involvement and positive collaboration.

You can almost always count on being able to identify at least one or two SMEs for the major functional areas within an enterprise. 

They are usually good candidates for involvement in Governance for your Models.
