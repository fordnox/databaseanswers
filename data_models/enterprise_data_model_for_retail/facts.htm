Enterprise Data Model for Retail 
Back to the Data Model 
This relates to an Enterprise Data Model for the Retail business.

The US Association for Retail technology Standards ('ARTS') has published a very interesting Data Model.

Oracle offers a Retail Model, with an excellent Tutorial.

IBM also offers a Retail Data Warehouse.


B. Barry says the Things of Interest are :-
1. Customers
2. Customers Sales
3. Products
4. Others to be determined

C. The Business Rules are as follows :-
1. A Customer can have zero, one or many Customer Sales.
2. Every Customer Sale must be associated with a valid Customer.
3. A Customer Sale is always associated with a Payment Method.
4. A Customer Sale can have one or many Products.
5. An In-Store Purchase is always associated with a member of Staff.
