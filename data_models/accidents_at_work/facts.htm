Facts for an Accidents at Work
 

A Data Model is required by the Health & Safety Manager.

The FACTS define the Business Rules ...

1. What are the 'Things of Interest' in the Scope to be modelled ...

  • Accidents
  • Accident Types
  • Employees
  • First-Aid Officers.
  • Locations
  • Treatments
  • Victims

2. How are these 'Things of Interest' related ? ...

  • ACCIDENTS occur to VICTIMS in LOCATIONS.
  • An ACCIDENT must be of a certain ACCIDENT_SERIOUSNESS.
  • An ACCIDENT HISTORY can be maintained for each EMPLOYEE.
  • The ACCIDENT HISTORY can identify the common ACCIDENTS.
  • The ACCIDENT HISTORY can identify the TRAINING required by FIRST-AID OFFICERS.
  • A TREATMENT can be administered for an ACCIDENT.

3. What do we know about the 'Things of Interest' ? ...

  • AN ACCIDENT will always have an ACCIDENT DESCRIPTION.
  • A VICTIM has a Name, Address and Other Details.
  • A VICTIM is either an EMPLOYEE, or an EMPLOYEE_ASSOCIATE, or a VISITOR.
