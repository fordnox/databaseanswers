The Draft Business Rules have been defined and are listed below. 
A. The Area being Modelled is :-
A CVs and Job History.

B. The Things of Interest, ('THINGS'), include :-
B.1 CVs
B.2 Jobs
B.3 Job History
B.4 Skills

C. These THINGS are Related as follows :-
C.1 A CV consists of one or many Jobs.
C.2 A Person has one and only one Job History.
C.3 A Job History consists of many Jobs.
C.4 A Person can have zero, one or many CVs.
C.5 A Job has zero, one or many Skills.

D. Other Characteristics of these THINGS include :-
D.1 A Job has a Start Date and an End Date.
