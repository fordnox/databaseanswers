The Area being Modelled is :-
Pharma and Biotechnology Information 

Hi:

My homework assignment is to create a Data Model for a pharma and biotechnology information for 
drug and medical device developers. 

The database will combine data from many sources on the various actors and resources in 
the pharmaceutical industry.


The goal is to provide a list of desired subjects (hospitals, clinical trial sites, etc.), 
attributes (departments, drugs tested, etc.), relationships and design goals, and provide an optimal design. 

It is anticipated that there will be 10-20 primary subject tables (not counting intermediate tables, value tables, 
normalized tables, etc - with these we suppose there may be on the order of a hundred tables actually implemented) 
and some tens of attributes for each subject.

I look forward to hearing from you.

Thanks a lot.
