Data Model for Product Complaints Data Mart

Back to the Top-Level Model.

The purpose of this Data Mart is to bring together all the data required to produce Reports about Product Complaints, and no other data.
This makes it possible for anyone wanting a Report to say "Give me these totals (ie the Facts) by these Dimensions".

For these reasons, a Data Mart is also called a Dimensional Model.
