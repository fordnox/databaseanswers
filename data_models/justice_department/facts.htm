FACTS for the Data Model for Justice Department
 
  • Things of Interest in the Scope of the Data Mode :-
    1. Articles.
    2. Authors.
    3. Correspondence.
    4. Correspondence Elements.
    5. Correspondence Personalization Options.
    6. Departments
    7. Employees.
    8. Organizations.
    9. Positions.
  • What are the relationships between these Things of Interest :-
    1. A piece of CORRESPONDENCE can contain one or many Correspondence Elements.
    2. Each CORRESPONDENCE ELEMENT can have zero or many Personalization Options.
    3. An EMPLOYEE can participate and have EMPLOYEE_MEMBERSHIP in zero or many ORGANIZATIONS.
    4. The EMPLOYEE_MEMBERSHIP will be associated with one particular INVOLVEMENT_TYPE.
    5. The EMPLOYEE_MEMBERSHIP will be associated with one particular MEMBERSHIP_ROLE.
    6. The EMPLOYEE_MEMBERSHIP will be associated with one particular MEMBERSHIP_LEVEL.
    7. Each ORGANIZATIONS can have zero or many EMPLOYEES as MEMBERS.
  • What are the characteristics of these Things of Interest :-
    At present, the Model shows only the ID field, but in practice, each Entity will have many attributes, for example ...
    1. A DEPARTMENT has a NAME.
    2. An EMPLOYEE has a NAME.
    3. An ORGANIZATION has a NAME. Etc..
