Migration Strategy

The Approach

The Approach is :-
1. Identify our Plan for the future, that is the 'To-Be'
2. Determine our starting-point, that is the 'As-Is'
3. Establish our Strategy, in discussion with the users,
    to get from the As-Is to the To-Be in a planned
    and controlled manner.
Conceptual Data Model

The Purpose

The purpose of this Model is to provide a starting-point for
discussion with the Users to establish the Requirements.
It is normally completed in the first week of a new assigment.

There are three simple Steps to completion of this Model.

These are as follows :-
Step 1. Establish the 'Things of Interest'
Step 2. Define how they are related.
Step 3. Review the conclusions with users
            to achieve consensus and Sign-off.
