Demo of User Queries for a Customer
 

This is a demonstration of a Glossary of Business Terms with a User-Friendly Query facility.

BUSINESS TERM SOURCE DESCRIPTION
Customer Gen A Customer is a person or individual that the Group does or might do business with.
Customer A A Customer corresponds to the Generic definition.
This System is likely to use Siebel, where a Customer is a combination of Account and Contact.
Customer B A Customer is Registered User of the Web Site.
This System uses BroadVision, where a Customer is a combination of USER and USER_PROFILE.
Customer C A Customer is yet to be defined.
This System is likely to use Siebel, where a Customer is a combination of Account and Contact.
