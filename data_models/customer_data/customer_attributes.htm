The Attributes of a Generic Customer
 

This is a demonstration of a Glossary of Business Terms.

ENTITY ATTRIBUTE DESCRIPTION
Customer customer_id A unique number, automatically generated whenever a new Customer is created.
  customer_name Name.
  customer_title Title.
  gender Valid values are Male, Female or Undetermined.
  date_of_birth  
  home_owner_yn Is the Customer a Home Owner ? Valid values are Yes, No or U(nknown)
  years_in_residence For CRM , how many years in residence at present address.
  years_in_residence For CRM , how many years as a Customer of the Group.
