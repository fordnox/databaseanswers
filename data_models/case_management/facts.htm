The Draft Business Rules have been defined and are listed below. 
The Area being Modelled is :-
Law Enforcement Case Management.

A. DESIGN NOTES :
A.1 This is a very general design, based on People and Events, and could be applied to 
many other kinds of Events involving People taking different Roles.

B. The Things of Interest, ('THINGS'), include :-
B.1 People
B.2 Associates of Prisoners(also referred to as Guests and Visitors).
B.3 Cases
B.4 Defendents
B.5 Events

C. These THINGS are Related as follows :-
C.1 A Person can be a Defendant in zero, one or many one Defendant Events.
D. Other Characteristics of these THINGS include :-
D.1 A PERSON has a DATE_OF_BIRTH and a GENDER.
