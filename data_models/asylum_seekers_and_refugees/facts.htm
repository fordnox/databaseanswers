Refugess seeking Asylum in the UK.



A. DESIGN NOTES :
A.1 The Design reflects the Life Cycle of a Refugee who is claiming Asylum ...
This has been drafted as :-
Stage 1) Enter the UK
Stage 2.1) Make Application upon Entry to UK - may be removed to Safe Third Country.
Stage 2.2) Make Application after Entry.
Stage 3) Go through the Screening Process
Stage 4) Appear for Interview
Stage 5) Home Office Integrated Casework Directorate Decision :-

    

  1. 3.1 Granted Refugee Status

  2. 3.2 ELR/ELE

  3. 3.3 Refusal


4.1 Allocated to approved Accommodation.
4.2 If refused, then removed from the UK.
