Data Model for the Analysis of Unicef Activities   
We would be glad to have your comments.
For this Model we used our UN Activities Model as our Design Pattern. 
This Tutorial is intended for students and other people who 
are interested in Data Modelling and learning about Unicef activities.

It combines a 'Self-Study' course in Data Modelling with techniques 
in information analysis to answer these four questions :-
1. What Activities does Unicef have in progress ?
2. How many people are involved in each Activity ?
3. What is the budget for each Activity ?
4. Where does the money come from ?
When I got started on this Project it became clear that Unicef was very advanced in data processing and
therefore it would be be an excellent vehicle for a Tutorial for young people or anybody interested
in learning how to analyse data and produce performance reports.
